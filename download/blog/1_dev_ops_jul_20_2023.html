10 Steps for Insurance DevOps Transformation

Insurance carriers, owing to strong compliance, they have complex build process and that make it slower for carriers to deploy a simple or complex build in to production

The use of AI and ML in DevOps will continue to grow in 2023, particularly in automated testing, continuous deployment, and monitoring. DevOps teams can leverage these technologies to optimize and automate workflows, improving software development efficiency.

As cyber threats evolve, carriers CSIO increasingly prioritize security and compliance. DevSecOps is well-suited to meet these demands

DevOps as a Service (DaaS) is a new trend where third-party providers offer DevOps services to carriers. DaaS allows companies to outsource the management of their DevOps infrastructure and processes, reducing the operational burden on their internal teams. This emerging trend is gaining momentum, and more organizations are considering DaaS as a viable option.
